📊 YSL Crypto Research — Senior Analyst Workspace

Institutional-quality crypto research and wealth intelligence, built for high-net-worth investors who are new to digital assets.


🧭 Overview
This repository supports the research and content operations of a Senior Crypto Research Analyst at Yieldschool (YSL) — a crypto investment education and advisory community. The work here is purpose-built for a specific audience: high-net-worth individuals (HNWIs) aged 50–70, most of whom are approaching crypto for the first time after decades of success in traditional finance, real estate, or business.
Everything produced here is written and designed around one core question:

"How would I explain this to a successful 60-year-old executive who has never owned crypto?"

That single standard shapes every analysis, every newsletter, every report, and every tool in this workspace.

👤 Role & Responsibilities
1. 📰 Weekly Newsletter
A weekly member newsletter covering macro and crypto developments — written in plain English, free of jargon, and always contextualized within broader wealth preservation themes. Each edition includes:

Key Takeaways — A single, flowing paragraph summarizing the week's most important developments
Macro Market Recap — Economic conditions, geopolitical developments, and how they affect crypto
ETF & Regulatory News — What's moving in institutional and regulatory circles
Crypto Fund Flows — Where institutional money is moving
Asset Updates — Performance and developments across covered assets
Research Hub Highlights — Featured project analyses from the research database
Top Movers — Notable price action explained in plain terms
Upcoming Live Calls — What's coming up for members

Formatting rules (standing):

Key Takeaways = one flowing paragraph, not bullet points
All bullet annotations = one tight sentence maximum
The "Actionable Insights" section is never edited
Preview text always uses the advisor tone: calm, clear, leads with context, ends with a reason to read


2. 🔬 Crypto Research Dashboard
A comprehensive, single-file HTML dashboard containing institutional-quality analyses of 75+ crypto projects, maintained and updated on an ongoing basis. Each project analysis follows a strict 11-section template:

Key Metrics (Price, Market Cap, FDV, TVL where applicable)
Launch and Milestones
Sector and Competitive Position
Project Financials and Tokenomics
Token Value Accrual Mechanisms (mandatory)
Risk Report (includes GoPlus flags, Rugcheck findings, wallet concentration, hacks, legal injunctions)
Significant Partnerships (mandatory)
Users and Traction
Product Market Fit
Accessibility
Summary Verdict (includes explicit risk rating and recommended position sizing)

Covered sectors: DeFi, Layer 1 & Layer 2 Blockchains, DePIN, AI/Crypto, RWA (Real World Assets), BTCFi, Liquid Staking, Launchpads, Decentralized Social, and more.

3. 📅 Weekly Live Call Content
Two recurring weekly deliverables supporting YSL's live educational calls:

One-liner descriptions — Concise, benefit-first descriptions for upcoming live calls, written to communicate what members will gain and what profit-oriented action they can take
Call summaries — Bolded date headers followed by ~4 concise sentences: macro/BTC context, main educational topic, featured yield plays, and a closing note or warning


4. 📋 Weekly Posts (x2)
Two short, punchy posts per week covering the most important things clients need to know. Written to be read in under two minutes with no prior crypto knowledge required.

5. 📆 Monthly Report
A structured, professionally formatted monthly report covering macro and crypto highlights and insights. Suitable for direct client distribution.

6. 🏛️ Research Hub Management
Ongoing maintenance and updates to the YSL Research Hub — a dark-themed, embeddable member resource featuring:

Live price feeds (CoinGecko API, refreshed every 60 seconds)
Curated asset profiles with external data links
Contract addresses with copy-to-clipboard functionality
AI-powered Q&A panels with pre-written, cached responses for the most common client questions:

"What is [asset] and why is it recommended?"
"What are the key risks?"
"What are the current price targets?"
"How much of the portfolio should be in [ticker]?"
